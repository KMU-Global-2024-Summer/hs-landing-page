Problem Statement

When educational materials and information online become outdated, FUP detects and alerts users, reducing learning or research disruptions and improving the internet user experience.

Our Solution

FUP ensures reliability and personalization in the information flow, saving valuable time and providing easy maintenance, thereby enhancing social connections and keeping up with trends and market dynamics.

Validation of the Problem

Users face confusion with outdated information, such as old health advisories or legal documents. FUP's timely updates provide reliability and prevent misinformation.

Market Opportunity

With a TAM encompassing all online platforms and a serviceable market in the millions, FUP's potential market share is significant, given the universal need for updated information.

Competitive Landscape

FUP stands out with its automated, real-time updates and personalized services compared to Google Alerts, Talkwalker Alerts, Brand24, and Awario, setting a new standard in information accuracy and user experience.
